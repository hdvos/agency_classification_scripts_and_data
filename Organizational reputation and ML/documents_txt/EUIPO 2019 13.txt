UIPO staff engagement wins gold at the IC Brilliance Awards



The EUIPO’s internal video news channel, EUIPOnews, has won a Gold Award in the Brilliance in Employee Engagement category at the Internal Communication Brilliance Awards, organised by 
According to the : ‘The judges commended this entry because of its innovative approach to involving colleagues directly in the communication process. The result is a genuine and sincere fresh channel.’
EUIPOnews is a monthly video news programme designed and produced internally for and with the participation of staff, effectively supporting the internal communication and staff engagement of the Office.
