SATCEN DEPUTY DIRECTOR GIUSEPPE D'AMICO SPEAKS AT THE OPCW
On 12 November 2019 an EU SatCen delegation headed by its Deputy Director, Giuseppe D'Amico visited the Organisation for the Prohibition of Chemical Weapons (OPCW) in The Hague in the Netherlands.
The EU SatCen is supporting the OPCW inspectors within the framework of  the cooperation between the European Union and the OPCW, fully aligned with the  EU global strategy.
European Union Heads of Mission accredited to the OPCW received presentations concerning the overall functioning and scope of activities of SatCen as well as representative examples of the analytical products provided by SatCen to OPCW.
