ESAR KTN, Engage: Get involved in 2020
Dec. 10, 2019
Engage has announced a range of dates and opportunities for getting involved in its programme in 2020:
The cybersecurity workshop will take place on 17 June 2020 (hosted by Frequentis, Vienna)
The 2020 summer school from 06 July (hosted by EUROCONTROL, Luxembourg).
The second Call for proposals supported by catalyst funding will open in January 2020.
These opportunities are open to all. Institutions may also advertise positions for researchers on the Engage website, so do watch that space after the ER4 Call outcome is announced. For each activity, follow the link under ”.
