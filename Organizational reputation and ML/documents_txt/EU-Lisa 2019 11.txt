eu-LISA Industry Roundtable October 2019 Report 
On 17 October 2019, the European Union Agency for the Operational Management of Large-Scale IT Systems in the Area of Freedom, Security and Justice (eu-LISA) hosted its 11th Industry Roundtable “Towards Practical Implementation of the New JHA Information Architecture” in Tallinn, Estonia.

The event was dedicated to discussing solutions for the implementation of the new interoperability architecture that are aligned with legal, functional and technical requirements.

The Industry Roundtable convened over 100 participants from 51 different organisations in 19 countries, representing the global border management and security industry, EU Member States, the United States of America, Canada, the EU institutions and JHA Agencies as well as academia.
