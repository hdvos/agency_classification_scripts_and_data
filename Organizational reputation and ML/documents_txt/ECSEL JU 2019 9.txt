Vacancy notice for a traineeship programme within ECSEL JU

ECSEL Joint Undertaking is looking for a trainee to join the team. The traineeship programme, operated within the ECSEL JU, gives trainees an understanding of the Joint Undertaking and its role within the activities of the European Union, enables them to acquire practical knowledge of the ECSEL JU and its regulatory framework and to obtain professional experience in the course of their work.
ECSEL JU offers on-the-job training opportunities in the different units, for candidates with background or career development goals in the mentioned topics:
Administration
Operations
Communication
