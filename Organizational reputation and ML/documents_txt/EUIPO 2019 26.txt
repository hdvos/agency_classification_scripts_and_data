Ecuador joins DesignView



 
As of 11 November 2019,  (SENADI) has made its design data available to the DesignView search tool.
With the addition of more than 2 600 industrial designs from SENADI, DesignView provides information and access to more than 14.8 million designs in total. DesignView now contains data from 70 participating offices.
Since the introduction of DesignView on 19 November 2012, the tool has served more than 5.1 million searches from 163 different countries, with the UK, Germany and China among the most frequent users.
The integration of SENADI in DesignView is an activity of the IP Key Latin America programme directed by the European Commission and executed by the EUIPO.
