DELEGATION OF BELGIAN CIRCLE OF POLICE LEADERSHIP VISITS CEPOL HEADQUARTERS 
You are here
 »  »  » Delegation of Belgian Circle of Police Leadership visits CEPOL Headquarters 

24 October 2019
On 24 October, CEPOL's Executive Director, Dr. h. c. Detlef Schröder, welcomed a delegation of Belgian Circle of Police Leadership to our premises as part of their study visit to Hungary.
Visitors were presented with CEPOL's training portfolio, Agency's latest achievements, and future goals in the area of law enforcement training.
Additionally, representatives of the Hungarian police presented the work of the Hungarian police and some of the challenges they face to their Belgian colleagues.
The Delegation continued their study visit in Hungary today by visiting the International Training Centre in Budapest and the border crossing between Hungary and Serbia.
