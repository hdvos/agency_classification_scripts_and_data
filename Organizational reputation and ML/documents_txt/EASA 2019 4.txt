Easy Access Rules for Air Traffic Controllers’ Licensing and Certification (Regulation (EU) 2015/340) updated
Related Content


EASA has updated the Easy Access Rules for Air Traffic Controllers’ Licensing and Certification (ATCO), incorporating all technical requirements and administrative procedures.

It covers all annexes of Regulation (EU) 2015/340, and the Executive Director Decisions related to them, including the ED Decision 2019/023/R updating of the ATCO initial training content.

The Implementing Rules (IRs) are displayed in a consolidated, easy-to-read format, followed by the related Acceptable Means of Compliance (AMC) and Guidance Material (GM), offering advanced navigation features through links and bookmarks.

The document will be updated regularly to incorporate further changes.

Thank you for sending us your feedback and comments to erules@easa.europa.eu.
